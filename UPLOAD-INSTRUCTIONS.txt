MR & MRS WHEELER SNEAK-PEEK WEBSITE

This folder is complete and already contains all 26 unique photographs.

UPLOAD TO GITHUB:
1. Open the cassar-wedding-gallery repository.
2. Tap Add file, then Upload files.
3. Upload every item inside this folder:
   - index.html
   - styles.css
   - script.js
   - README.md
   - .nojekyll
   - the complete images folder
4. Commit the changes to the main branch.
5. Wait approximately 1–3 minutes.
6. Visit:
   https://cassarcreativephotography.github.io/cassar-wedding-gallery/

No names, captions or watermarks are placed on the photographs.
